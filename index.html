Fun Facts About Camels

  • Camels have 2 eye lids, one of them are transparent so that the camel can see with its eyes closed in a sand storm.
  • Camels used to be the most used animals for transportation of goods in Africa and Saudi Arabia.
  • Camels store water in the bumps on their backs to survive in the dry deserts.

Fun Facts About Pigeons

  • Did you know that pigeons can move their wings 10 times per second.
  • Pigeons can fly at the speed of 50 to 60 miles per hour.
  • Pigeons can have a heart beat of 600 beats per minutes.
